THREAT INTELLIGENCE BULLETIN
Subject: BlackMatter Variant Targeting Healthcare — Active Campaign
Classification: TLP:AMBER
Date: 2026-06-10

OVERVIEW
========
A ransomware campaign targeting healthcare and pharmaceutical organizations has
been observed across three confirmed victims in North America and Europe.
The actor appears to be a BlackMatter affiliate operating independently following
that group's shutdown. Ransom demands range from $2M to $8M USD.

INITIAL ACCESS VECTOR
=====================
All three victims were compromised via RDP brute-force attacks against
internet-exposed Remote Desktop servers. The actors used credential stuffing
lists compiled from prior healthcare-sector breaches.

Observed attacker source IPs:
  - 194.165.16.78
  - 194.165.16.79
  - 194.165.16.80
  (Three consecutive IPs — likely same /24 VPS block)
  - 45.9.74.33 (separate campaign, same actor per infrastructure overlap)

DEFENSE EVASION
===============
- Disabled Windows Defender on all compromised hosts:
  Set-MpPreference -DisableRealtimeMonitoring $true
  Set-MpPreference -DisableIOAVProtection $true
- Deleted volume shadow copies:
  vssadmin.exe delete shadows /all /quiet
  wmic shadowcopy delete
- Cleared Windows event logs:
  for /F "tokens=*" %1 in ('wevtutil.exe el') DO wevtutil.exe cl "%1"
- Ransomware binary signed with stolen code-signing certificate:
  Subject: CN=DigiSoft Solutions Ltd, O=DigiSoft Solutions Ltd, C=RO
